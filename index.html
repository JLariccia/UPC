We’re like the sugar in cookies…

Even though you can’t see the sugar, it wouldn’t be a cookie without it. United Plastics Corp. manufactures custom vinyl plastics that are used as parts in larger, more complex products or systems. And while the UPC product isn’t always visible (like the sugar in a cookie), it plays a critical role in the functionality and performance of the end product. Our plastics are made into parts for doors, windows, roofing, floor mats and more, and offer a range of benefits including protection, soundproofing, and aesthetics.
